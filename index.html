About Ariana Vale

Ariana Vale writes paranormal romance for women who understand that magic doesn't fix everything —but it sure makes life more interesting.

After years of wondering why fantasy heroines were always twenty-two, she decided to write the books she wanted to read: stories about fierce women in their forties who solve problems with intelligence, experience, and the occasional magical argument with reality.

The Midlife Weave is her debut novel and the first book in the Weave Arbiter series.
